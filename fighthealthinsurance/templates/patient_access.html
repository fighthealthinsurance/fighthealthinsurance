Workflow Integration

Human review is optional. Choose the workflow that fits your compliance requirements and team capacity. All options maintain full audit trails.

Fully Automated

Patient self-serve. Appeals generated and delivered without manual intervention. Your team monitors outcomes.

Human Reviewed

Appeals flagged for review before delivery. Your specialists approve, edit, or escalate as needed.

Co-Authored

Patient starts the appeal with AI assistance. Your specialist finishes and adds clinical context.

Approval Gates

All appeals require explicit approval before delivery. Full control for high-stakes or regulated contexts.

Your team stays in control. These workflows augment your patient access specialists. They don't replace them. Choose the level of oversight that matches your regulatory requirements.

How Teams Use This

Pharmaceutical Hub Team

A SKYRIZI hub team deploys a denial-specific landing page for patients who receive "step therapy required" denials. The page is pre-populated with:

  • Approved clinical evidence for IL-23 inhibitors
  • Common payer policy references for step therapy exceptions
  • Guidance on documenting prior treatment failures

Hub specialists review flagged appeals and add patient-specific clinical context before the appeal is finalized.

Hospital Market Access Team

A hospital market access team standardizes appeal evidence across their health system. They configure:

  • Consistent messaging for prior authorization denials
  • System-wide clinical evidence libraries
  • Outcome tracking by payer, denial type, and procedure

Appeals route through their patient financial services team, who add patient-specific documentation before submission.
