Evidence You Control

Your team decides what clinical evidence, citations, and arguments go into the context for each landing page.

✓ Peer-reviewed studies and citations — Include specific PubMed references, clinical guidelines, and trial data that support medical necessity.

✓ Payer-specific policy arguments — Reference the payer's own coverage criteria and explain how the patient meets their requirements.

✓ Internal clinical evidence — Upload your organization's approved clinical messaging and real-world evidence.

✓ Branded clinical messaging — Consistent voice and approved language across all patient-facing appeal content.

Workflow Integration

Human review is optional. Choose the workflow that fits your compliance requirements and team capacity.

Fully Automated

Patient self-serve. Appeals generated and delivered without manual intervention. Your team monitors outcomes.

Human Reviewed

Appeals flagged for review before delivery. Your specialists approve, edit, or escalate as needed.

Co-Authored

Patient starts the appeal with AI assistance. Your specialist finishes and adds clinical context.

Approval Gates

All appeals require explicit approval before delivery. Full control for high-stakes contexts.

Your team stays in control. These workflows augment your patient access specialists. They don't replace them. Choose the level of oversight that matches your regulatory requirements.
