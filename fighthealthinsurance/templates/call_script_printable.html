Tips for the call:
  • Take notes as you go — you will need them for the written appeal.
  • Ask for the name, title, and a reference number for every person you speak with.
  • Ask the insurer to send written confirmation of anything they tell you.
  • Calls are recorded by the insurer; you may record your own copy in one-party-consent states.
  • If the call goes off-script, return to your numbered questions before hanging up.
