Other Resources

There are many other resources for fighting for a fair outcome with your health insurance provider.

Government Resources

State & Local Advocates
  • Many states offer health insurance advocates to help navigate appeals
  • Example: CA Health Advocates for California residents
Medicare Assistance
  • State Health Insurance Assistance Programs (SHIP) - free Medicare counseling
Federal Resources
  • Healthcare.gov - general appeal information
  • CMS.gov - detailed appeal guidance

Condition-Specific Resources

Organizations focused on specific conditions often provide the most relevant and detailed guidance.

  • JDRF - Diabetes resources and advocacy
  • Cancer Support Community - Cancer-related appeals
  • National MS Society - Multiple Sclerosis resources

These resources are often created by people in similar situations and can provide highly relevant guidance.

General Resources

While general-purpose websites may not be as specific to your situation, they can provide helpful overviews:

  • WebMD - General appeal guidance
  • Fight Health Insurance (this site) - AI-powered appeal assistance
