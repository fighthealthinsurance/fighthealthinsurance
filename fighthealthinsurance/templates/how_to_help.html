Follow us on social media and help raise awareness about the importance of appealing unfair insurance denials. Every voice makes a difference!

You can also help us improve by using our Chooser Interface to contribute anonymized data about insurance denials.

Contribute to Open Source Healthcare Tools

Fight Health Insurance is fully open source. We welcome contributions from developers, designers, and anyone who wants to help improve the project. The stack includes Django, React/TypeScript, and Ray for distributed processing.

Check out our repositories, report issues, or submit pull requests — every contribution helps patients fight unfair denials.
