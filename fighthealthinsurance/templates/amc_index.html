Why Appeal Your Denial?

Insurance denials are common, but many are overturned on appeal. Studies show that over 50% of denied claims are reversed when patients appeal. Don't accept "no" as the final answer.

What You'll Need

To create the most effective appeal, have the following ready:

  • Your denial letter - the document from your insurance company explaining why your claim was denied
  • Your email address - we'll send you a copy of your appeal
  • Any supporting documents (optional) - medical records, doctor's notes, or other relevant information

Know Your Rights

  • You have the right to appeal any denied insurance claim
  • Insurance companies must provide a reason for denial
  • You can request an external review if internal appeals fail
  • Time limits apply - most appeals must be filed within 180 days

Appeal Success Rates
